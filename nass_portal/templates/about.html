Nigerian Army School Of Signals

NASS stands as a beacon of professionalism, innovation, and strategic communication within the Nigerian Army.

Our Journey

The Nigerian Army School of Signals is a key institution within the Nigerian Army responsible for training personnel in the field of communication and information technology. It plays a crucial role in developing the skills and knowledge of soldiers to effectively handle modern communication systems, signal equipment, and information management.

Our Mission

To produce well trained and proficient personnel for the NA in the fields of Combat Communications, Electrical/Electronics Engineering, Telecommunications, ICT and related fields.

Academic Excellence

Our academic programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the dynamic field of military signal operations. The curriculum covers telecommunications, information technology, cryptography, and electronic warfare.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

NASS boasts state-of-the-art facilities that simulate real-world scenarios, providing students with hands-on experience in signal operations.

Specialized Courses

From basic signal training to advanced courses, our programs cover the entire spectrum of signal operations, incorporating the latest technological advancements.

Research and Development

NASS actively engages in research and development activities, collaborating on projects to enhance military communication capabilities.

Community Engagement

NASS recognizes the importance of community engagement and strives to foster positive relationships with local communities through outreach programs and initiatives.
